About Us

Based in London, BC Collection is an innovative jewellery design business. With many unique pieces, BC Collection have direct access to a carefully curated collection of premium antique jewellery, specifically, natural pearls from the Victorian period of 1880 to 1930.
The company provides expert-led authentication services to assure each piece's historical and intrinsic value and a bespoke restoration and redesign service, customising antique jewellery to modern tastes without compromising its historical integrity.
